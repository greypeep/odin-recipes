Strawberry fondue

In this recipe we will learn how to make an amazing delicious strawberry fondue

Ingredients

  • 23-ounce container of frozen strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons of maraschino cherry juice.
  • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of cold water

Recipe

  1. Begin by placing the frozen strawberries in a food processor or blender, and mix until they are all pureed. Then, transfer the mixture to a medium saucepan.
  2. In a separate small bowl, combine the cornstarch and water, whisk and then stir it into the strawberry puree.
  3. Proceed to bring the strawberry mixture to a boil, cook and stir for 1-2 minutes while boiling or until thickened. Next, stir in the cherry juice and then remove from the heat.
  4. This fondue is wonderful warm, but let it cool for just a few minutes before transferring to a serving bowl.
